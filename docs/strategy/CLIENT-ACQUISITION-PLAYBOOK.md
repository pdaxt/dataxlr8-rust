# Client Acquisition Playbook — Land a Client Next Week

_Concrete, day-by-day action plan. No theory. Just do this._

_Updated: 2026-03-04_

---

## The Offer

**"$5K AI Quick Win — We replace your spreadsheets and manual workflows with AI agents in 1 week. If it doesn't save you 10+ hours/week, you don't pay."**

Why this works:
- **Low risk** — money-back guarantee removes friction
- **Fast delivery** — 1 week, not 3 months
- **Specific outcome** — "save 10+ hours/week" is measurable
- **Entry point** — once inside, upsell to $25K-75K core build

---

## Who to Target

### Ideal Client Profile (ICP)

| Attribute | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Company size | 5-50 employees |
| SaaS spend | $1K-5K/mo on tools (Salesforce, HubSpot, Apollo, etc.) |
| Pain | Manual data entry, spreadsheet hell, 5+ SaaS tools that don't talk to each other |
| Role | Founder, CEO, Head of Operations, VP Sales |
| Industry | Any — agencies, consultancies, SaaS startups, e-commerce, professional services |
| Geography | Global (English-speaking, timezone-flexible) |

### LinkedIn Sales Navigator Filters

```
Title: CEO, Founder, Managing Director, Head of Operations, VP Sales
Company size: 11-50 employees
Industry: Marketing & Advertising, IT Services, Management Consulting,
          Professional Services, SaaS, E-commerce
Keywords: "CRM" OR "Salesforce" OR "HubSpot" OR "spreadsheet" OR "manual process"
Posted in last 30 days: Yes (active on LinkedIn)
```

### Red Flags (Skip These)

- Enterprise (500+ employees) — sales cycle too long
- Pre-revenue startups — can't pay
- Companies already using AI agents — they've solved it
- Companies with in-house engineering teams — they'll build it themselves

### Wednesday — Outreach Batch 2 + Follow-Up (1.5 hours)

- [ ] Send 15 more LinkedIn connection requests
- [ ] Send 10 more cold emails

- [ ] Send 15 more LinkedIn connection requests
- [ ] Send 10 more cold emails
- [ ] Follow up with Wednesday connections

### Friday — Close + Follow-Up (1.5 hours)

- [ ] Follow up with all warm leads
- [ ] Send proposals to anyone who showed interest
- [ ] Take remaining discovery calls

- [ ] Write blog post: "We Replaced Clearbit with a 6.5MB Rust Binary"
- [ ] Prepare case study template (even if no client yet, use hypothetical)

## Discovery Call Script (15 min)

**Opener (2 min):**
"Thanks for taking the time, [Name]. I'll keep this quick — 15 minutes. I'd love to understand what tools your team uses day-to-day and where the biggest time sinks are. Then I'll share what we're building and see if there's a fit."

**Discovery (5 min):**
1. "What does your team's daily workflow look like?"
2. "What tools are you currently paying for?" (get specific: CRM, email, enrichment)
3. "What's the most annoying manual task your team does?"
4. "How much time does [that task] take per week?"
5. "What have you tried to fix it?"

**Pitch (3 min):**
"So here's what we do differently. Instead of connecting to Salesforce through an API, we REPLACE Salesforce entirely. Our tools are open-source, written in Rust, and 50x faster than anything on the market.

For your specific situation, I'd replace [their pain point] with AI agents that [specific outcome]. Based on what you told me, you'd save roughly [X hours/week and $X/month].

We do this as a $5K 'AI Quick Win' — we build it in 1 week. If it doesn't save you [their stated hours], you don't pay."

**Close (5 min):**
- "Does that sound like it would solve [their problem]?"
- "We can start as early as [next Monday]. Want to lock in a spot?"
- If hesitant: "No commitment needed today. I'll send a 1-page proposal with exactly what we'd build. You can decide by [day]."

## What You Actually Build for Quick Win Clients

### Package A: Lead Enrichment + CRM ($5K)

For teams doing manual lead research:
- Deploy enrichment-mcp (email verification, company lookup)
- Deploy crm-mcp (contacts, deals, pipeline)
- Connect to their existing email
- Set up AI agent that auto-enriches new leads
- **Outcome:** 10+ hours/week saved on lead research

### Package B: Email Automation ($5K)

For teams sending manual follow-ups:
- Deploy email-mcp (templates, sequences, tracking)
- Deploy crm-mcp (contact management)
- Set up AI-generated follow-up sequences
- **Outcome:** 5-8 hours/week saved on email, 2x response rates

### Package C: Reporting + Dashboard ($5K)

For teams building reports in spreadsheets:
- Deploy analytics-mcp (KPIs, dashboards)
- Deploy crm-mcp (pipeline data)
- Set up automated daily/weekly reports via AI agent
- **Outcome:** 5+ hours/week saved on reporting

